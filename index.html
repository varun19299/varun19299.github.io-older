Welcome!

I’m a senior undergraduate in the Department of Electrical Engineering at the Indian Institute of Technology Madras (IIT-M), India. My goal is to pursue a PhD in the intersection of computer vision and computational photography, begining Fall 2020.

This website is a collection of my research projects and other exploratory projects done. There is also a solitary blog-post regarding a pet research idea of mine: CNN-SLAM. Hopefully, there’ll be a few others joining this one soon!

In case any of these topics piques your interests and you would like to talk about them (or just say hi) feel free to contact me!
